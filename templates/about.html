🪔 Who We Are

Guardians of Sacred Tradition

Kashi Ganga is a devotional service organization based in Varanasi (Kashi) — the oldest continuously inhabited city in the world and the spiritual capital of India. Our team of experienced Vedic priests has been conducting sacred Ganga Aarti ceremonies for over 15 years.

We believe that every life milestone — a wedding, a birthday, an anniversary — deserves a sacred dimension. The holy Ganga is not just a river; she is the divine mother who washes away sins and bestows blessings upon all who approach her with devotion.

Our mission is to make authentic, accessible, and deeply meaningful Vedic ceremonies available to all families — whether you visit Varanasi or we come to your location.

🏛️ The Sacred City

Why Varanasi?

Varanasi (also known as Kashi or Benares) is one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities and the holiest site in Hinduism. Located on the banks of the Ganga, it is believed that dying here leads directly to liberation (moksha).

The Ganga Aarti performed here daily is a ritual that has continued uninterrupted for thousands of years. Participating in or witnessing this ceremony is considered a profoundly transformative spiritual experience.

When you book a ceremony with us, you are not just booking a service — you are connecting with an ancient living tradition that has blessed millions across millennia.
