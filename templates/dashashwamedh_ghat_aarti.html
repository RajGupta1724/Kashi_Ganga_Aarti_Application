Dashashwamedh Ghat Aarti

Learn about the legendary Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, Varanasi’s most iconic riverfront.

History of Dashashwamedh Ghat

Dashashwamedh Ghat is one of the oldest and most significant ghats in Varanasi, believed to be created by Lord Brahma to welcome Lord Shiva. The name means “the ghat of ten sacrificed horses.”

Importance of the Aarti

The evening Ganga Aarti here is a grand spiritual event, attracting thousands of devotees and tourists daily. Priests perform rituals with lamps, incense, and chanting, creating a mesmerizing atmosphere.

What to Expect

  • Vibrant rituals with music and fire
  • Spiritual ambiance and crowd energy
  • Best views from boats or front row seats
