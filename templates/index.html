❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About Booking Ganga Aarti

How do I book a private Ganga Aarti?

Use the booking page or WhatsApp us with your preferred date, occasion, and ghat. We will confirm the timing and ritual details.

Can you arrange Ganga Aarti for weddings?

Yes. We organize Wedding Ganga Aarti ceremonies for families who want a sacred blessing at Assi Ghat, Dashashwamedh Ghat, or a private venue.

Do you provide birthday and anniversary ceremonies?

Yes. Birthday Ganga Aarti and Anniversary Ganga Aarti are both available with custom planning for families, couples, and visiting guests.

Which ghats are available?

We commonly arrange ceremonies at Assi Ghat and Dashashwamedh Ghat, depending on the ceremony style, crowd conditions, and timing.

Can you handle corporate or custom events?

Yes. Corporate Ganga Aarti and private custom ceremonies can be planned for teams, guests, and special family occasions.

Do all images need alt text?

Yes. Descriptive alt text improves accessibility and gives search engines more context about each ceremony image and landing page.
