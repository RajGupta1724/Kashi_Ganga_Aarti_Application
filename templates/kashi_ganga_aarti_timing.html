Kashi Ganga Aarti Timing

Discover the exact timings for the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti in Varanasi. Plan your visit to witness this spiritual spectacle at the right moment.

Ganga Aarti Timings (2026)

  • Summer (April–October): 6:30 PM – 7:30 PM
  • Winter (November–March): 6:00 PM – 7:00 PM

Morning Aarti

Starts at 5:00 AM daily at Dashashwamedh Ghat and Assi Ghat.

Evening Aarti

Begins at sunset, usually between 6:00–7:00 PM depending on the season.

Best Time to Visit

Arrive at least 45 minutes early for the best view. Weekends and festivals are more crowded.
